Best Places to Visit in Kashmir

Gulmarg

Experience the thrill of the Himalayas with our exclusive travel package to Gulmarg. Known for its stunning landscapes and adventurous activities, Gulmarg is a paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Gondola Ride: Enjoy a breathtaking ride on the Gulmarg Gondola, the highest cable car in the world. Marvel at the panoramic views of the Himalayas and the lush green meadows below.

Skiing and Snowboarding: Indulge in thrilling skiing and snowboarding adventures on the powdery slopes of Gulmarg. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Gulmarg offers something for everyone.

Golfing: Tee off at the highest golf course in the world, surrounded by the stunning Himalayan peaks. The Gulmarg Golf Course is a must-visit for golf enthusiasts.

Alpine Treks: Explore the picturesque alpine meadows of Gulmarg on a trekking adventure. Witness the beauty of colorful flowers, gurgling streams, and snow-capped peaks.

Local Culture: Immerse yourself in the rich culture and heritage of Gulmarg. Interact with the friendly locals, taste delicious Kashmiri cuisine, and shop for traditional handicrafts.
