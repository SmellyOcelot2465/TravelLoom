Best Places to Visit
Kuala Lumpur

The capital city of Malaysia is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic skyscrapers, vibrant street markets, and diverse culinary scene. Key attractions include the Petronas Twin Towers, Batu Caves, Kuala Lumpur Tower, and the bustling shopping districts of Bukit Bintang and Chinatown.
Langkawi

Langkawi is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can relax on beaches like Pantai Cenang and Tanjung Rhu, take a cable car ride to the top of Gunung Mat Cincang for panoramic views, and explore attractions like the Langkawi Sky Bridge and Underwater World Langkawi.
Cameron Highlands

The Cameron Highlands is a hill station known for its cool climate, lush tea plantations, and scenic landscapes. Visitors can take guided tours of tea estates like Boh Tea Plantation, visit strawberry farms and butterfly gardens, and explore hiking trails in the Mossy Forest.
