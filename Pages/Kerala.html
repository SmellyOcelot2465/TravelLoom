Best Places to Visit in Kerela
Alleppey

Famous for its picturesque backwaters, Alleppey is a must-visit destination in Kerala. You can experience a memorable houseboat cruise through the serene backwaters, passing by coconut groves, paddy fields, and small villages.
Kovalam

This beach town is famous for its pristine beaches, lighthouses, and Ayurvedic resorts. Kovalam Beach, Lighthouse Beach, and Hawa Beach are popular spots for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports.
Kumarakom

Another enchanting backwater destination, Kumarakom is renowned for its scenic beauty and bird watching opportunities. You can explore the Vembanad Lake, visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, and indulge in Ayurvedic treatments at luxury resorts.
